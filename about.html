Why we exist

The energy transition deserves better than legacy search.

The biggest hires in this market are too important to hand to a generalist and a junior team. Yet that is exactly what the legacy model delivers: a senior partner who pitches, then disappears, and a process that can’t keep up with the speed of market conditions. Net Zero Search does the opposite. We work only in the markets we know, the partner who wins your mandate is the partner who fills it, and we move at the speed the transition demands. Sector depth makes that speed possible.

The Edge

Built on all four. Not one or two.

Most search firms can offer you one of these. Maybe two, if you’re lucky. We’re the only firm built on all four.

01

Real, deep sector experience

Deep sector-specific experience and networks since 2010. Genuinely rare. Most ‘specialist’ recruiters arrived in the green economy because the sector got fashionable. We have been there from the early days. You’re not paying for someone learning on the job.

02

A partner runs your search, start to finish

No handover, nothing lost along the way. The person who understood your brief is the same person shortlisting candidates. With most firms that’s two different people, and something always gets lost between the partner who sold the search and the junior researcher delivering it.

03

Fees tied to the outcome, not just the shortlist

Most premium search firms bill a third up front, a third on shortlist, a third on start date. That means two-thirds of their fee is locked in before anyone has actually started the job. Ours is structured around the hire working out, not just landing.

04

We are business owners

We’re business owners, not just recruiters. We understand your P&L, your growth plan and your hiring risk the way another business owner would, not just the job spec. And because it’s our business and our money, every search gets the level of care you’d expect if you were paying us out of our own pockets. Because you are.

It’s not any one of these that makes the difference. It’s having all four, together, that no other search firm can match.
