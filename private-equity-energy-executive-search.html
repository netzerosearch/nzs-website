FAQ

Common questions.

What types of investors do you work with?

Private equity, infrastructure funds, venture and growth equity, and the family offices and institutional LPs backing direct energy transition & digital infrastructure deals. Different capital structures, different hold periods, different exit expectations, and we work all of them.

Which roles do you place at the fund level?

Managing Partner, Partner, Investment Director, Managing Director, Principal, VP of Investment, Operating Partner, Capital Formation and Investor Relations. People originating and executing deals, or adding operational value across a portfolio, not the operators running the businesses afterwards. We also place investment & corporate development professionals at platform level.

Do you only work buy-side, or do you cover sell-side too?

Both. On the Sell-side we work with investment banks, advisories, and bulge bracket and boutique banks running the M&A and financing processes on transactions across the sector.

Do you place investment and finance specialists at platform level too?

Yes. There's often movement between fund and portfolio company, someone spends a few years on the deal team, then moves in-house to run finance for a platform, or the reverse. Platform companies often carry funding structures the fund itself doesn't, project finance, debt, mezzanine, tax equity, and need people in-house who actually understand them. We hold relationships with those specialists on both sides of the table, not just the fund.

Which geographies do you cover?

North America, UK, Europe, Middle East and APAC. Where the mandate sits, one of our Managing Partners covers it personally.

How fast can you deliver a shortlist?

Two to four weeks to a first shortlist, with 98% of retained searches filled.
